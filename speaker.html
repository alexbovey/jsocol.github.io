James Socol

James lives in Brooklyn, N.Y. where he works as Mozilla's Community Platforms Manager. He is responsible for the development of several major web properties as well as the growth of the engineering teams and the engineers themselves.

James has given talks at conferences, meetups and summits on topics ranging from continuous delivery to scaling web applications to the value of Open Source software, and is interested in speaking on these topics and others including engineering culture and web application security.
